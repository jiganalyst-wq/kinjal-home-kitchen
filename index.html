Spinach, Avocado Smoothie with Banana

Breakfast Fuel

Spinach + basil + avocado + banana blended with coconut milk, lemon, chia, and ice. Topped with soaked falooda (subza) seeds. Cold, filling, clean.

  • ⏱ ~10 min
  • 🥬 Spinach + basil
  • 🧊 Makes ~3 glasses
Ingredients
  • 1½ cups spinach (torn)
  • 1 cup basil
  • ¼ cup coconut milk
  • ¼ cup roughly chopped avocado
  • ½ cup roughly chopped banana
  • ½ tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp chia seeds
  • 2 tbsp soaked falooda/subza seeds
  • 1 cup water
  • 20 ice cubes
Steps
  1. Soak 2 tsp falooda/subza seeds in 2 tbsp water for ~10 min.
  2. Add spinach, basil, coconut milk, avocado, banana, chia seeds, lemon juice to blender.
  3. Add 1 cup water + 20 ice cubes.
  4. Blend till completely smooth.
  5. Pour into 3 glasses and top with the soaked falooda seeds.
  6. Serve immediately.

Papaya Orange Drink

Vitamin C Boost

Fresh orange juice + ripe papaya + thick coconut milk. Blend, then pour over crushed ice. Summer in a glass.

  • ⏱ ~5 min
  • 🍊 No added sugar
  • 🥭 Hydrating
Ingredients
  • 2 cups fresh orange juice
  • 2 cups roughly chopped papaya
  • ½ cup thick coconut milk
  • Crushed ice (for serving)
Steps
  1. Blend orange juice, papaya, and coconut milk until smooth.
  2. Fill each glass with crushed ice.
  3. Pour the drink over the ice.
  4. Serve immediately.
