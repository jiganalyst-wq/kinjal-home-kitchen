🥬 Spinach, Avocado Smoothie with Banana

Breakfast Fuel

Spinach + basil + avocado + banana blended with coconut milk, lemon, chia, and ice. Topped with soaked falooda (subza) seeds. Cold, filling, clean.

  • ⏱ ~10 min
  • 🥬 Spinach + basil
  • 🧊 Makes ~3 glasses
Ingredients
  • Spinach
  • Basil
  • Avocado
  • Banana
  • Coconut milk
  • Chia seeds
  • Falooda (subza) seeds
  • Lemon juice
  • Water + ice
Steps
  1. Blend everything until smooth.
  2. Top with soaked falooda seeds.
  3. Serve cold immediately.

🍊 Papaya Orange Drink

Vitamin C Boost

Fresh orange juice + ripe papaya + thick coconut milk. Blend, then pour over crushed ice. Summer in a glass.

  • ⏱ ~5 min
  • 🍊 No added sugar
  • 🥭 Hydrating
Ingredients
  • Fresh orange juice
  • Ripe papaya
  • Thick coconut milk
  • Crushed ice
Steps
  1. Blend orange, papaya, and coconut milk until smooth.
  2. Pour over crushed ice.
  3. Serve immediately.

🫘 Mixed Pulses with Vegetables

Protein Bowl

Moong, kala chana, and masoor tossed with onions, blanched cauliflower, baby onions, garlic, and a Kashmiri chili–coriander–cumin paste. Bright lemon at the end.

  • ⏱ ~25 min
  • 🫘 Fiber + protein
  • 🌶 Chili + garlic flavor
Ingredients
  • Moong (whole green gram)
  • Kala chana (brown chickpeas)
  • Masoor (red lentils)
  • Onion, baby onions
  • Blanched cauliflower
  • Garlic, ginger
  • Kashmiri red chilli
  • Coriander + cumin seeds
  • Lemon juice, salt
Steps
  1. Cook pulses until tender.
  2. Blend garlic, chilli, coriander, cumin, ginger with water to make a paste.
  3. Sauté onions and cauliflower, add pulses and paste.
  4. Season with salt and lemon juice. Garnish with coriander.
